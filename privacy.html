Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

GOOD VIBES AGENCY ("GVA") operates GVA Analytics, an internal tool for managing TikTok analytics for talent and client accounts.

1. Information We Collect

Via TikTok API (with user consent):

  • TikTok Open ID and display name (user.info.basic)
  • Profile info: bio, verification status (user.info.profile)
  • Account stats: follower count, likes, video count (user.info.stats)
  • Video performance: views, likes, comments, shares (video.list)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Performance analytics reports for authorized GVA staff
  • Content strategy optimization for influencer campaigns
  • Monthly performance reports for clients and talent partners

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Data is accessible only to authorized GVA staff.

4. Data Retention

Analytics data is retained for up to 12 months. Tokens can be revoked anytime via TikTok settings.

5. Security

We use encrypted storage for access tokens and restrict access to authorized personnel only.

6. Your Rights

Revoke access anytime via TikTok app settings. For data deletion requests: info@goodvibesagency.tokyo
